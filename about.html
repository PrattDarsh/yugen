About us

Yugen is an organization that was born from a lifetime of work, learning & passion for education and we translate this to creating life-long learning communities with the adult groups in schools.

Mission Objectives SDGs Stakeholders Our Stories Community

Our
Vision

Our vision is a world where the education system is fair and inclusive where teachers are lifelong learners, ensuring high-quality education to all students irrespective of their social and economic background leading to a fairer and socially equitable society.

Our social mission is to create equitable, innovative and lifelong learning communities for teachers and school leaders and, through them, improve the quality of learning in schools. Currently, we are working with rural public school teachers and school leaders of Tamil Nadu.

Our
social mission

Our key objectives

To make schools a safe place and enriching by honing the social and emotional skills of teachers and students.

Help teachers bridge gaps in math learning through data-driven classroom interventions.

Help school leaders become community leaders by developing relational leadership skills.

To bring cutting-edge developments in education across the globe to the school adult community.

To build empathy through design thinking training to local bureaucrats and school leaders.

To build the key objectives for scale by leveraging affordable technologies and local languages.

To co-create a culture of learning and development among the school adult community.

Our SDGs and targets

What are sustainable development goals?

The Sustainable Development Goals are a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet and improve the lives and prospects of everyone, everywhere. The 17 Goals were adopted by all UN Member States in 2015, as part of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development which set out a 15-year plan to achieve the Goals.
