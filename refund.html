Refund and Cancellation Policy

Yugen Education Foundation is a not-for-profit organization. We are grateful for your donation and support of our organization. Yugen Education Foundation will apply users’ donations in accordance with its internal policies and procedures.

Our policy on refund and cancellation of donations received for Yugen Education Foundation’s activities on a secure online payment gateway is as under:

> Donations are non-refundable.

> Yugen Education Foundation accepts book donations and uses them in one of their projects or sends them to rural school libraries. Due to logistic constraints, all donations of books to Yugen Education Foundation are not eligible for cancellation and refund.

> We, therefore, request you be sure before you donate.
