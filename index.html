About Us

Yugen is an education-centered organization born from a lifetime of work, learning, and passion. Our goal is to create communities of life-long learning for both kids and adults affiliated with schools.

Donate

We believe that the best way to transform the caliber of education in our schools is by providing educators with high quality resources. And one of the simplest & most effective ways to give back to your community is to support the ongoing efforts of those working at the ground level.

Every bit of support translates exponentially into access to quality education in our country’s schools. In spaces such as these, even a little bit goes a long way.
